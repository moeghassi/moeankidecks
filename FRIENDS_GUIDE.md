# Get the shared French A1.2 vocabulary card deck

This only takes a few minutes. You need Anki on a Mac or Windows computer for
the initial setup.

## 1. Install Anki

Download Anki Desktop from the official website:

<https://apps.ankiweb.net/>

Choose the download for your computer, install it, and open Anki.

## 2. Download the shared deck

Click this link to download the shared French A1.2 vocabulary card deck:

<https://github.com/moeghassi/moeankidecks/releases/download/syncaddon-v0.1.1/moeankidecks.ankiaddon>

Your computer will download a file named:

```text
moeankidecks.ankiaddon
```

## 3. Install the shared deck

1. Open the downloaded `moeankidecks.ankiaddon` file.
2. Anki will open and ask you to confirm the installation.
3. Close and reopen Anki when the installation finishes.

The vocabulary cards will download automatically after Anki opens.

## 4. Find the deck

On Anki's main screen, look for:

```text
+ Moe Shared
```

Click the **+** to expand it. You will then see **A1.2**. Click **A1.2** to
start studying.

## Getting new cards later

New cards are checked automatically whenever you open Anki Desktop. You can
also check manually using:

**Tools → Sync Moe Anki Decks**

Your study progress is kept. Existing cards are not reset or deleted.

## Using the deck on your phone

The initial setup and future card updates happen through Anki Desktop, but the
cards can still be used on a phone. Sign in to AnkiWeb and press **Sync** in
Anki Desktop, then sync AnkiMobile or AnkiDroid with the same account.
